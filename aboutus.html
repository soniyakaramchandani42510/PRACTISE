CUC is a group of buddies from the 2018 entrance batch of Electrical Engineering Department, IIT(BHU). From a formal HVEE group in 1st sem to this time, CUC has evolved not as a group but as a family, where we all live, study and enjoy together. We are a group of friends always excited to explore new things.
